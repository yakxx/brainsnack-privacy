Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

BrainSnack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

• Email address — used for authentication

• Username — displayed in the app and leaderboard

• Usage data — lessons completed, XP earned, streaks, badges

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

• Provide and maintain the BrainSnack service

• Track your learning progress and streaks

• Display leaderboard rankings

• Send daily streak reminder notifications (only if you enable them)

• Improve our app and content

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), which complies with industry-standard security practices. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Notifications

BrainSnack may send local push notifications to remind you to maintain your daily learning streak. You can disable these at any time in your device settings.

5. Biometric Authentication

If you choose to use biometric login (fingerprint or Face ID), this data is processed entirely on your device. We do not store or have access to your biometric data.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

• Google Firebase — authentication and data storage (Privacy Policy)

• Google AdMob — advertising (coming soon) (Privacy Policy)

7. Children's Privacy

BrainSnack is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access your personal data

• Delete your account and all associated data

• Opt out of notifications at any time

To delete your account or request data deletion, contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
